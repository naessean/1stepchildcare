Toddler Program

Each of our Toddler Classrooms are licensed for nine children with 2 teachers. Children are offered plenty of opportunity for exploration, discovery, and learning on their own terms through free play—paired with a structured play-based curriculum.

Classroom Age Ranges

Our toddler program is grouped to match each child’s stage of development.

Classroom Age Range
Infant–Toddler 15 months – 2 years old
Toddler 2 years old – 2.6 years old
Toddler–Preschool 2.6 years old – 3.3 years old

Program Details

In our Toddler Program, children are offered plenty of opportunity for exploration, discovery and learning on their own terms through free play.

Through free play, we promote interaction and social skills through socialization with peers, playing in a group, and sharing and taking turns.

In partnership with free play, children also learn through a structured play-based curriculum where they engage in literature, sensory, dramatic play, art, STEM, and movement activities to learn about a subject that teaches children more about themselves, others, or the world around them.

Through a combination of structured play and free play, children enhance development, while also growing independence, self-esteem, and confidence.

Free Play Exploration, discovery, and learning on their own terms
Social Skills Group play, sharing, taking turns, and peer interactions
Structured Curriculum Literature, sensory, dramatic play, art, STEM, movement
Confidence Building Independence, self-esteem, and confidence

We also ensure to

  • Review classroom rules daily
  • Encourage and reward appropriate behavior to further boost self-esteem and confidence
  • Provide an environment that is stimulating, yet safe and not overly challenging
