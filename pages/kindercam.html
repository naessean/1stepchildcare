KinderCam

Secure, real-time viewing that allows families to stay connected to their child’s day.

What Is KinderCam?

Childcare centers across the nation hear “thank you” every day from Moms and Dads who take advantage of the value and convenience of KinderCam.

KinderCam is the industry leader in childcare viewing systems. Their state-of-the-art camera systems allow parents an effective way to see their child from home or work.

KinderCam uses any computer connected to the Internet and requires no software, unlike other child viewing systems.

Security & Peace of Mind

  • Protected, secure internet service
  • Authorized parent-only access
  • Access to assigned classrooms only
  • Security protections at the center and server level

Why Families Love KinderCam

For Parents

KinderCam allows parents to follow their child’s daily routine, watch interactions with peers, and stay connected to their child’s development.

Parents often make fewer calls to the center because they can see their child throughout the day.

For Children

Children benefit from a consistent, transparent environment where families feel confident and connected.

For Our Center

KinderCam strengthens the relationship between our staff, families, and children—supporting long-term trust and loyalty.
