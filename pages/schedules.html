Infant Schedule

  • Individual feeding and nap routines
  • Tummy time and sensory exploration
  • Music, books, and one-on-one interaction
  • Diapering and care as needed

Infant schedules are flexible and based on each child’s needs and family routine.

Toddler Schedule

  • Morning free play & guided activities
  • Circle time, songs, and movement
  • Snack, lunch, and rest time
  • Outdoor play and creative exploration

Toddlers follow a predictable routine that supports independence and social development.

Preschool / Pre-K Schedule

  • Learning centers and small group instruction
  • Early literacy, math, and science activities
  • Outdoor play and gross motor development
  • Lunch, rest, and afternoon enrichment

Our preschool schedule balances academics, play, and social-emotional learning.
