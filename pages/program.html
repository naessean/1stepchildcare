Programs for Ages 3 Months – 5 Years

First Step is a play-based learning center where children experience learning through play in literature, sensory, science, mathematics, dramatics, and physical activity.

How We Support Your Child

A consistent routine, caring educators, and learning through play—every day.

Play-Based Learning

Children explore and learn through hands-on activities across core areas like literacy, science, and math.

Positive Guidance

We support children in developing self-control by guiding each child in a positive way.

Center + Parent Teamwork

We believe in close collaboration between the center and parents to support each child’s development.

What to Expect

Our goal is for children to feel secure, loved, and excited to learn—while families feel confident and supported.

  • Daily routines that help children feel safe and confident
  • Learning through play across multiple classroom areas
  • Positive support for social-emotional skills and self-control
  • Ongoing communication and partnership with families
